◈ WELCOME TO FIREWALL — CONTEXT
Every player character is a sentinel — an agent-on-call for Firewall, a shadowy network dedicated to protecting transhumanity and counteracting x-risks. ◈ SETTING — PATHFINDER CITY & MA'ADIM RESEARCH PARK
Pathfinder City is the fastest-growing habitat in the solar system — glommed onto the wall of Gusev Crater, 40 km from the Martian Gate. Low polycarbonate domes over breathable (if low-pressure) atmosphere. New skyscrapers pierce the dome at the crater wall, giving top floors an unfiltered Martian horizon view. Under its shiny veneer: its own secrets.
Ma'adim Research Park surrounds the gate itself — pressurized canyon, armed to the teeth. Herzog Security has tagged and authorized everything within 25 km. Decontamination chambers, research labs, defensive systems aimed both outward and inward at the gate. The PCs are not the only ones after Nkomo — Herzog investigators are also working the disappearances. Their leads are cold, but if they hear about the PCs' investigation, they may take renewed interest.
Herzog, Pathfinder's security contractor, are smart and well-equipped. Infiltrators shouldn't expect meek resistance from their infosec specialists. Getting in a stand-up firefight with them is unwise.

BASILISK STARE
ACTION: TASK · DURATION: SUSTAINED · INFECTION MOD: 6
Eye contact with the target immobilizes them. As long as gaze is locked (minor blinking aside), they cannot move, avert their eyes, or undertake any action (including mesh and mental). Nkomo must also limit his movement — half base move and/or quick physical actions, with REF Check to maintain eye contact. If target is attacked, moved, or jostled, eye contact is lost. Cannot affect extra targets unless he has more than one set of eyes.
BROWSE THOUGHTS
ACTION: COMPLEX · DURATION: SUSTAINED · INFECTION MOD: 4
Scans the target's surface thoughts — general sense of what's running through their mind. Superior successes provide deeper detail. Not a full Deep Scan — just a read on current thoughts. Used paranoid surveillance before any interaction.

◈ GM NOTES — SESSION STRUCTURE
The session runs as a classic investigation with a social entry point (Hang Slaoui, the Heavenly Hand) followed by a crime scene (Espada's residence) and a nightlife circuit (Sticky Fingers → Frisky Business → Outland → 1851) that builds the picture of both Mardones and the pattern of disappearances. The trail intentionally points to Mardones as the killer — with Nkomo kept innocuous-seeming for as long as possible.
PACING NOTE The horror pivot is the 2-person airlock. Everything before it is detective work — methodical, social, satisfying. The moment a PC is alone with Nkomo in a sealed two-person airlock, the genre changes. The Basilisk Stare is the signal. Do not telegraph it — let the players think they have the situation contained before Nkomo makes his move.
If the gut eater escapes Nkomo and attempts a new host, the scene becomes containment horror. The PCs are in an airlocked maze, one or more may be fighting for their body, and Lixia Lok may be watching from outside — deciding whether to help or clean up.
Lok should not feel like an ally. Even if she assists, her assistance should feel like being studied by something vastly better-resourced and completely unaccountable. The PCs should leave this session with the uncomfortable feeling that Ozma got more out of this encounter than they did.
